## Internship Program
Prompt Engineering Internship  
**Organization:** Future Interns

---

## Task Title
Custom Chatbot Persona Creator – Build AI Personalities with Prompt Engineering

---

## Objective
The objective of this task is to design and document multiple AI chatbot personas using prompt engineering principles.  
The project focuses on defining chatbot tone, behavior, conversational flow, and safety boundaries, along with deploying one working chatbot using a no-code platform.

---

## Task Description
In this task, three distinct chatbot personas were designed for real-world use cases such as mental wellness support, academic tutoring, and travel planning.

Prompt engineering techniques were used to control:
- Chatbot tone and personality
- Behavioral boundaries and safety handling
- Conversational flow and fallback logic

One chatbot persona was deployed using a no-code chatbot builder to demonstrate a working implementation.

---

## Chatbot Personas Designed
1. **CalmSpace – AI Mental Wellness Companion**  
   Focused on emotional support, stress relief, and empathetic conversation.

2. **StudyMate – AI Subject Tutor**  
   Designed to help students understand concepts using simple, step-by-step explanations.

3. **TripWise – AI Travel Guide**  
   Helps users plan trips with destination suggestions, budgets, and itineraries.
